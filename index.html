How Moddle works

Getting your APK or mod is dead simple. Here's the whole process — no surprises, no tricks.

search

Search or browse

Use the search bar to find any app by name, or browse by category. Results show both APK and any available mods side by side.

touch_app

Tap the app

Tap any app card to open its detail sheet — description, mod features, ratings, and the complete version history from v1.0.0 to today.

verified_user

VirusTotal verified

Every file is scanned across 70+ antivirus engines before going live. You'll see the badge right on the download button.

download

Download directly

Hit download and watch the progress popup. When it's done, the file opens automatically. No timers, no redirects.

install_mobile

Install on Android

Open the APK on your device. Android may ask to allow "Install from unknown sources" — that's normal and safe for verified files.

block

Zero spam, always

No popups, no ads on tap or scroll, no autoplay. Moddle keeps it clean so nothing ever gets in your way.
